Installation

To add the University Header to a page on your site, add the following script tag to the head of your document.

<!-- ADD UCF HEADER -->
<script type="text/javascript" src="http://universityheader.ucf.edu/bar/js/university-header.js"></script>
<!-- END UCF HEADER -->
